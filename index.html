A Historical Timeline
of the Founding, 1819

  1. 28 Jan 1819

    Arrival off the island

    Raffles anchors his survey expedition off the mouth of the Singapore River, judging the harbour ideal for a free port.

  2. 29 Jan 1819

    The landing

    Raffles steps ashore and opens negotiations with the Temenggong, Abdul Rahman, the island's resident Malay chief.

  3. 06 Feb 1819

    Treaty signed

    A formal treaty with Sultan Hussein Shah and the Temenggong grants the East India Company the right to establish a trading post.

  4. 07 Feb 1819

    Departure & first Resident

    Raffles sails on, leaving Major William Farquhar as the settlement's first Resident and Commandant.
